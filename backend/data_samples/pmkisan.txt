PM-KISAN Scheme

The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) is a Central Sector Scheme that provides income support to landholding farmers' families across the country. Under this scheme, financial benefit of Rs.6000/- per year is provided to eligible beneficiary farmers' families in three equal installments of Rs.2000/- each every four months.

Eligibility:
- Small and marginal farmers with combined landholding/ownership of upto 2 hectares
- The benefit will be provided to families defined as husband, wife and minor children
- Farmers who are income tax payees are excluded
- Farmers holding constitutional posts, government employees, pensioners are excluded

Required Documents:
- Aadhaar card
- Bank account details
- Land ownership documents (revenue records)
- Mobile number for SMS alerts

Application Process:
Farmers can apply online through pmkisan.gov.in portal or visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC). The scheme is implemented through State/UT Governments and Union Territory Administrations.

Benefits:
- Direct cash transfer of Rs.6000 per year
- Three installments of Rs.2000 each
- Money transferred directly to bank account
- No intermediaries involved

Contact:
For queries, farmers can contact PM-KISAN helpline at 155261 or visit district agriculture office.
